## Overview

This playbook provides a step-by-step methodology for assessing, planning, and executing cloud migration programmes. It is designed for enterprise environments with legacy infrastructure and regulatory constraints.

## Who This Is For

Technology leaders, enterprise architects, and programme managers responsible for cloud transformation.

## Key Topics

- Migration assessment frameworks
- Workload classification and prioritisation
- Cost modelling and business case development
- Risk management for regulated environments
- Post-migration optimisation strategies
