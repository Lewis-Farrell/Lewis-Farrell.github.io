Most relationships reveal their true character within 90 days. The initial period of heightened emotion fades, and what remains is the actual dynamic between two people.

If you want to understand whether a relationship has long-term potential, pay close attention to what happens after the first three months. Do you still enjoy spending time together when there is nothing exciting happening? Do small conflicts get resolved or do they accumulate?

The 90-day test is not about reaching a deadline and making a decision. It is about paying attention to the trajectory. Relationships that are getting better at 90 days are qualitatively different from those that are merely coasting on initial chemistry.
