There is a critical difference between attraction and selection. Attraction is the initial pull toward someone. Selection is the decision to invest in them.

Most dating advice focuses on attraction — how to be more attractive, how to generate interest. But the more important skill is selection. Choosing the right person to invest in matters far more than being maximally attractive to everyone.

Good selection requires clarity about your own values and goals. It requires the ability to distinguish between someone who excites you and someone who is genuinely compatible with your life. These are not always the same person.
